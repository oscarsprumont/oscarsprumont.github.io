About me

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at the University of Washington, where I am fortunate to be advised by Anup Rao. I am broadly interested in theoretical computer science, with a particular emphasis on complexity theory and coding theory. In particular, I have recently been very interested in understanding what general properties of a code ensure good properties on the symmetric and erasure channels.
